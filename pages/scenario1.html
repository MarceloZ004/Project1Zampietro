Scenario 1

Scenario 1: Virtual Tour Planning

Alex wants to experience a virtual tour of the museum.
He accesses the museum's website on his tablet.
Views the virtual tour option on the website.
Selects a specific exhibit from the virtual tour menu.
The museum offers a 360-degree view of the exhibit halls.
Interactive objects in the exhibit offer detailed information when clicked.
Alex enjoys learning more through video and audio narrations.
Alex completes the tour and receives a recommendation for related exhibits.
He saves the recommendation to view later.
Decides to share the virtual tour link with friends.
